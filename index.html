Welcome to our lab

Identity, Diversity, Emotion, and Leadership

The IDEAL Lab at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo aims to promote positive outcomes for diverse groups in employment, work, and classroom settings. We study how cultural factors such as identity, diversity, or emotional expression may subtly and unconsciously shape our behaviors, judgments, and decisions.
